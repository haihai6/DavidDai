My Projects

Dragon Oath (天龙八部)

An action-adventure MMORPG where I served as a core designer. I was responsible for combat system design, level pacing, and game balance adjustments. I designed diverse combat systems and skill progression mechanics to ensure players continuously experience fresh content throughout their journey.

This game helped ChangYou successfully list on NASDAQ. It has been profitable for over a decade, making it one of the most successful MMORPG games in China with annual revenues of several billion RMB.
